# Linear Algebra Through Geometry - Syllabus

## Week 1

- Introduction to linear algebra and applications.
- Matrices and operations.
- Systems of linear equations and visualizing systems.
- Homogeneous systems.

## Week 2

- Gauss elimination.
- RREF, matrix inverse.
- Determinants and Cramer's rule.

## Week 3

- Vectors in 2D.
- Length, linear independence, dot product.
- Orthogonal projections.
- Lines in 2D, linear maps.
- Eigenvalues and eigenvectors in 2D.

## Week 4

- Vectors in 3D.
- Cross product.
- Lines and planes.
- Distance between points, lines, and planes.

## Week 5

- Linear maps in 3D.
- Volumes and linear maps.
- Eigenvalues and eigenvectors in 3D.

## Week 6

- Linear systems in 3D.
- LU decomposition.
- Least squares.
- Gram-Schmidt orthonormalization.
- QR decomposition.

## Week 7

- Vector spaces and subspaces.
- Linear combinations.
- Basis and dimension.

## Week 8

- Fundamental subspaces.
- Rank and nullity.

## Week 9

- Least squares and best optimal solution.
- Pseudoinverse.

## Week 10

- Real symmetric matrices.
- Properties and quadratic forms.

## Week 11

- Singular value decomposition.
- Applications of linear algebra.

## Week 12

- Image compression.
- Principal component analysis.
